    Hi everyone!

    This post is to ask all of you for any recordings from concerts or any other Coldplay live experience, in order to create "unofficial" albums.

    In any case take the work, the respective credits will be given, and as clarifications:
    1. Preferably be from the "Ghost Stories, A Head Full Of Dreams or Music Of The Spheres" tours.
    2. My goal is to be able to create albums for publication and for my own use.
    3. It can be a live broadcast (as Global Citizen), Direct recording (as a spectator in the stadium).
    4. I request that please be something recorded by you, as it will be a content that will be used for new Coldplay projects I need the approval of each one.

    As an extra request, please send your files without any type of compression, this to have the best quality possible, if it is also in your availability, please send data to identify (example: "Coldplay at the River Plate on October 29th 2023", any other information will be appreciated).

    You can send your files to:
    - Private message in SM
    - Telegram: @RetroBeatFM
    - Email: retrobeatfm@outlook.com

    I thank all those who make a small contribution to this project.
