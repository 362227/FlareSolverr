    The Silent Hill franchise is characterized by its instrumentals, the work of the great Japanese musician Akira Yamaoka. These reflect the melancholy, sadness and despair that surround their characters as they feel trapped in this nightmarish world.

    Silent Hill: The Otherworld Tunes is a compilation of 42 instrumental tracks composed by Yamaoka, Daniel Licht (tracks 1, 9, 22 and 36) and Jeff Danna (track 38) extracted from the soundtracks of Silent Hill, Silent Hill 2, Silent Hill 3, Silent Hill 4: The Room, Silent Hill: Origins, Silent Hill: Homecoming, Silent Hill: Shattered Memories, Silent Hill: Downpour, Silent Hill: Book of Memories, Silent Hill: Revelation and Silent Hill Sounds Box. The cover was designed by @shtheotherworld.
