    Joey Lawrence     is the debut album by American singer and actor Joey Lawrence, released on February 2, 1993, on MCA Records.

    Three singles were released from the album, "Stay Forever," "I Can't Help Myself," and "Nothin' My Love Can't Fix", co-written by Lawrence.

    1. I Can't Help Myself
    2. Nothin' My Love Can't Fix
    3. Stay Forever
    4. Justa 'Nother Love Song
    5. Night by Night
    6. I Like the Way (Kick Da Smoove Groove)
    7. In These Times
    8. Anything for Love
    9. My Girl
    10. Where Does That Leave Me
    11. The Ways of Love
    12. Read My Eyes
