    "Seven Sins" (stylized in all-caps) is the third single album by DRIPPIN. It was released on April 19, 2023 with "Seven Sins" serving as the single's title track.



    Member Alex did not participate in this comeback as he is on hiatus since January 26, 2023.[1]

    The physical album comes in two versions: Red and Dark.

    Background
    On March 23, 2023, Woollim Entertainment released a concept trailer for DRIPPIN, hinting an upcoming comeback in April.[2] On April 5, a teaser poster was released, confirming that the group would be releasing their third single album, "Seven Sins", on April 19.[3]



    Track list
    1. "Seven Sins" - 3:35
    2. "Stereo" - 3:45
    3. "Bad Blood" - 2:50
