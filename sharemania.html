    Silent Hill 4: The Room is a 2004 survival horror game developed by Team Silent, a group in Konami Computer Entertainment Tokyo, and published by Konami. The fourth installment in the Silent Hill series, the game was released in Japan in June and in North America and Europe in September. Silent Hill 4 was released for the PlayStation 2, Xbox, and Microsoft Windows. Its soundtrack was released at the same time. In 2012, it was released on the Japanese PlayStation Network. On October 2, 2020, it was re-released on GOG.com with patches to make it playable on Windows 10.

    Unlike the previous installments, which were set primarily in the town of Silent Hill, this game is set in the southern part of the fictional city of Ashfield, and follows Henry Townshend as he attempts to escape from his locked-down apartment. During the course of the game, Henry explores a series of supernatural worlds and finds himself in conflict with an undead serial killer named Walter Sullivan.

    Silent Hill 4 features an altered gameplay style with third-person navigation and plot elements taken from previous installments. Upon its release, the game received generally favorable critical reaction, and its departure from the typical features of the series received a range of reactions.

    For unclear reasons, the PC version of the game released by Konami is missing a few apartment hauntings. The kitchen faucet, wandering slippers, peephole Henry, closet shadow, Walter portrait, and shabby dolls above the item chest are absent. However, these hauntings were restored through a mod in the GOG PC version.

    FEATURES:
    • Remastered graphics
    • 60 FPS 4K upscaled cutscenes
    • PlayStation 2 apartment hauntings
    • Plus everything included on Konami's original PC version
    Please, follow the steps below after you have downloaded the files inside the Silent Hill 4 - The Room (2004) [GOG Version] folder:
    1. Download Silent Hill 4 - The Room (2004) [GOG Version] and extract its contents.
    2. Install the game.
    3. Once installed, go to the desktop, right click on the game shortcut and open the game directory folder.
    4. Download Ultimate-ASI-Loader and extract its contents.
    5. Rename dinput8.dll to dsound.dll and move it to the game directory.
    6. Download SH4.Randomizer.0.2.1 and extract its contents.
    7. Move data and scripts folders to the game directory.
    8. Go to the scripts folder, open randomizer.ini and set everything to 0, except for RestoreHauntings which must remain set to 1.
