    Unforgiven     (stylized in all caps) is the first studio album by LE SSERAFIM. It was released on May 1, 2023 with "Unforgiven" serving as the album's title track.
    The physical album will be available in ten versions: Dewy Sage, Dusty Amber, Bloody Rose, two Weverse Albums versions, and five member-specific Compact versions.

    Background
    On March 16, 2023, local news outlet Star News reported that LE SSERAFIM would be releasing an album in early May. Hours later, Source Music responded to the reports and stated that the comeback date would be revealed later.[1] On April 2, a video teaser posted to LE SSERAFIM's official YouTube channel announced the album's title, format and release date.[2]

    Commercial performance
    On April 11, YG PLUS revealed that Unforgiven had reached 1.03 million pre-orders.[3] On April 29, YG PLUS gave an updated statement and revealed that the album had reached 1.38 million pre-orders.[4]
