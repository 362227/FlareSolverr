    "You Better Hide" is a song by Swiss electronic band Yello featuring Swiss singer-songwriter Heidi Happy on vocals. Heidi Happy's real name is Priska Zemp, and she is a popular folk and jazz singer in Switzerland.

While many Yello songs are upbeat and danceable, "You Better Hide" showcases the duo's ability to create a slower, more contemplative track. The singer's hauntingly beautiful voice adds depth to the song's lyrics and helps convey the emotional message.
